Caring for your pets

Giving you peace of mind

Welcome to Bark & Stride - Dog walking!

As a dog owner, it can be difficult to find the time to give your furry friend the exercise and attention they need on a daily basis. That's where we come in.
Our team of experienced and reliable dog walkers are dedicated to providing top-notch care for your pup while you're away.
We are fully insured, which means that we have the necessary coverage in place to protect both your dog and our team while they are in our care.
We understand that your dog is a member of your family, and we will treat them as such.
We are committed to the safety and well-being of all the dogs in our care and take the responsibility of caring for your pet very seriously.

Services

In addition to our walking services, we also offer dog sitting and doggy daycare options for when you need a little extra help.
Our dog sitting services provide your dog with a safe and comfortable place to stay while you're away, whether it's for the day or an extended period of time.
Our doggy daycare services offer a fun and stimulating environment for your dog to play and socialize with other dogs while you're at work or otherwise occupied.
No matter what your needs are, we have a solution to suit you and your furry friend.

Booking

We understand that everyone's schedule is different, which is why we offer flexible scheduling to fit your needs.
Whether you need a daily walk for your dog or just a one-time visit, we can work with you to find a solution that works for you. Our competitive pricing means that you can get top-quality care for your furry friend without breaking the bank.
We serve North county Dublin and are always happy to meet new dogs and their owners.
If you're interested in booking a walk or any of our other services, don't hesitate to contact us today.
We'll be happy to answer any questions you may have and help you get started with your first booking.
